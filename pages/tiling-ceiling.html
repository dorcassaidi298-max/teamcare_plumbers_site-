FAQs

What is the typical lifespan of a tile roof?

The lifespan of a tile roof can vary depending on factors such as material, installation, and maintenance. On average, a well-maintained tile roof can last for 20-50 years.

How do I clean and maintain my tile roof?

Regular cleaning and inspection can help extend the life of your tile roof. We recommend cleaning your tile roof at least once a year and inspecting it for damaged or missing tiles.

Can you repair damaged ceiling tiles?

Yes, we offer ceiling tile repair and replacement services. Our team will assess the damage and provide a quote for the necessary repairs.
